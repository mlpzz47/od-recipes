Sponge Cake

Sponge Cake 👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍👍

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Yogurt
  • Sugar
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • Lemon Zest

Steps to Follow

  1. Separate the ingredients
  2. Mix eggs and sugar
  3. Then, oil, yogurt and lemon zest
  4. Then, flour, baking powder and salt
  5. Mix
  6. Put in a mould
  7. Bake 👍
